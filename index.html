Kaguya Love is War

Synopsis:

Kaguya-sama: Love is War (Japanese: かぐや様は告らせたい～天才たちの恋愛頭脳戦～) is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Aka Akasaka.
It’s published in English by Viz Media. Two spin-offs launched in 2018, and an anime adaptation by A-1 Pictures aired in 2019.

Summary:

Student council president Miyuki Shirogane and vice-president Kaguya Shinomiya appear to be the perfect couple. But both are too proud to confess their love, believing whoever confesses first loses. The story follows their clever (and hilarious) attempts to get the other to confess.
